PRESALE

Apollo's fair launch allows contributions to exceed the hard cap of 200 ETH. This results in tokens distributed proportionally as a function of oversubscription percentage with extra ETH refunded to contributors of the sale.

Example: An investor contributes 1 ETH and the final raise equals 400 ETH. This represents a 100% or 2x oversubscribed sale. Therefore 0.5 ETH is refunded to that investor and 0.5 ETH is contributed for $APOLLO tokens.

REFERRALS

For each person referred to the presale who contributes to the sale, a single point is given to the referrer. Once the sale has ended, all the points from all users are tallied and then referral rewards are distributed as a percentage of how many points a user has to the total number of points achieved in the sale.

EXAMPLE: If there is a total of 100 points collected during the presale and an individual user receives 5 points, then this user receives 5/100 = 5% of the total referral rewards.
